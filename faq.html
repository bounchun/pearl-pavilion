Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Reservations are highly recommended, especially on weekends and holidays. Limited bar seating is available for walk-ins.

Is there a specific dress code?

Smart casual is encouraged. Gentlemen should avoid shorts and athletic wear; refined denim is permitted.

Can you cater to dietary requirements?

Yes. Please mention any allergies or dietary preferences during booking, and our team will accommodate whenever possible.

Do you provide private dining options?

We provide a semi-private space ideal for small gatherings and business events. Please contact our team for availability and bespoke menus.
